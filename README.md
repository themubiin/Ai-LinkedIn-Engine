* Generates trending, high-impact LinkedIn content ideas using AI
* Researches industry-specific hooks and insights
* Writes posts in your authentic voice using GPT-4
* Designs scroll-stopping visuals with DALL·E
* Publishes consistently using LinkedIn API — no manual posting needed

## How It Works

### **AI Topic Discovery**

Finds trending and relevant topics based on your niche and audience.

### **Voice-Aligned Content Writing**

Generates full posts matching your tone, writing style, and goals.

### **AI-Generated Professional Visuals**

Creates polished images tailored to each post.

### **Fully Automated Scheduling**

Pushes content directly to LinkedIn via the LinkedIn API on your chosen schedule.
